# Editorial Policy

System Of All Story operates under structured editorial governance.



## Core Principles

- Accuracy
- Transparency
- Source verification
- Neutrality
- Clear labeling of sponsored content



## Fact-Checking Standard

Articles must:
- Cite publicly verifiable sources
- Avoid speculative claims presented as fact
- Distinguish clearly between reporting and commentary



## Corrections Policy

If factual errors are identified:

1. Editorial review is conducted.
2. Corrections are documented.
3. Updates are timestamped.

Retractions may occur if content fails verification standards.



## Crypto Coverage Standards

- No guaranteed return language
- No price predictions presented as certainty
- Disclosure of sponsored relationships
- Clear investment risk acknowledgment



## Medical & Neurological Coverage

- Reference peer-reviewed research where possible
- Avoid diagnostic claims
- Avoid presenting hypotheses as established fact



## Political Content

- Neutral tone
- Source-based reporting
- No endorsements presented as institutional position

System Of All Story does not operate as a political organization.
