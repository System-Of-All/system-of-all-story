# Subscription Model

System Of All Story provides tiered access to editorial content, research, and strategic insights. The subscription framework is designed to support responsible journalism, advanced analysis, and institutional‑level intelligence services.



## Tier 1 – Free Access

Available to all public readers.

Includes:
- General news coverage
- Basic cryptocurrency updates
- Public governance and policy reporting
- Access to non‑premium technology and world affairs content
- Limited access to Havana Syndrome informational articles

This tier is supported by advertising and open‑access editorial content.



## Tier 2 – Premium Access

Designed for readers seeking deeper analysis and structured insights.

Includes:
- In‑depth editorial analysis
- Long‑form research reports
- Weekly or monthly editorial newsletters
- Extended cryptocurrency and digital infrastructure coverage
- Early access to select investigative content
- Access to premium Havana Syndrome research summaries (non‑medical, informational only)

Premium subscribers support independent journalism and expanded research capabilities.



## Tier 3 – Institutional Access

Built for organizations, research groups, and professional entities.

Includes:
- Custom research reports tailored to organizational needs
- Strategic briefings on governance, digital systems, and global developments
- Priority access to editorial staff for structured inquiries
- Multi‑seat access for teams
- Early access to major reports and intelligence summaries
- Optional private briefings (non‑classified, publicly sourced analysis only)

Institutional access does not grant influence over editorial decisions.



## Subscription Principles

All subscription tiers follow these principles:

- No pay‑to‑influence editorial content
- No preferential treatment in reporting
- No access to classified, confidential, or non‑public information
- Clear separation between editorial independence and subscription revenue



## Payment & Compliance

- Subscription payments do not constitute investment or advisory services
- Crypto payments (if enabled) must comply with applicable regulations
- Refund and cancellation policies are defined in the Terms of Service
- Institutional clients may require additional compliance review



## Data & Privacy

Subscriber data is handled according to the platform’s Privacy Policy and GDPR‑aligned standards.

No subscriber data is sold or shared with advertisers.
