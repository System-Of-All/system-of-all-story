# Advertising Due Diligence Checklist

This checklist defines the mandatory verification steps required before approving any advertiser on System Of All Story. It applies to all advertising categories, with enhanced requirements for cryptocurrency, financial, and high‑risk sectors.

Completion of this checklist is required prior to publication of any advertisement or sponsored content.

---

## 1. Advertiser Identity Verification

### 1.1 Required Information
- Legal entity name  
- Registered business address  
- Corporate website  
- Contact person (name + role)  
- Official email domain (no free email providers)  
- Corporate registration number (if applicable)

### 1.2 Verification Steps
- Confirm domain ownership  
- Validate corporate registration  
- Confirm contact identity via business email  
- Check for prior fraud warnings or regulatory flags  

Status: ☐ Approved ☐ Rejected

---

## 2. Project / Product Verification

### 2.1 General Requirements
- Clear description of product or service  
- Public documentation or whitepaper (if applicable)  
- Transparent team information  
- Public roadmap or operational outline  

### 2.2 Red Flags
- Anonymous team with no corporate entity  
- Unrealistic claims or guaranteed outcomes  
- Lack of verifiable online presence  
- Conflicting or unverifiable information  

Status: ☐ Approved ☐ Rejected

---

## 3. Crypto‑Specific Due Diligence (If Applicable)

### 3.1 Technical Verification
- Smart contract audit (required for tokens)  
- Blockchain explorer link  
- Tokenomics transparency  
- Liquidity information (if relevant)  

### 3.2 Compliance Verification
- No guaranteed returns  
- No investment promises  
- No misleading terminology (“risk‑free”, “guaranteed yield”)  
- Jurisdiction‑appropriate disclaimers  

### 3.3 Risk Assessment
- Check for rug‑pull indicators  
- Review community reputation  
- Review past regulatory actions  

Status: ☐ Approved ☐ Rejected

---

## 4. Legal & Regulatory Review

### 4.1 Mandatory Checks
- Compliance with advertising laws in relevant jurisdictions  
- No violation of financial promotion regulations  
- No unlicensed financial services  
- No medical or diagnostic claims  
- No political campaign advertising  

### 4.2 Documentation
- Required disclaimers provided  
- Terms of service reviewed  
- Legal entity validated  

Status: ☐ Approved ☐ Rejected

---

## 5. Content Review

### 5.1 Advertising Material Requirements
- Accurate and non‑misleading  
- No exaggerated claims  
- No unverifiable statements  
- No prohibited content categories  
- Clear disclosure of sponsorship  

### 5.2 Editorial Review
- Tone and language appropriate  
- No conflict with editorial independence  
- No attempt to influence reporting  

Status: ☐ Approved ☐ Rejected

---

## 6. Financial Review

### 6.1 Payment Verification
- Valid payment method  
- No sanctions‑listed entities  
- No suspicious financial activity  
- No requests for undisclosed compensation  

### 6.2 Contractual Requirements
- Signed advertising agreement  
- Disclosure obligations accepted  
- Removal and enforcement clauses acknowledged  

Status: ☐ Approved ☐ Rejected
