# Privacy Policy

System Of All Story (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of our readers, subscribers, and partners. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard personal information in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

By accessing or using the platform, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate the platform, provide services, and improve user experience.

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- Email address (for subscriptions or newsletters)
- Account information (if applicable)
- Payment information (processed through third‑party providers)
- Voluntary communications (support requests, feedback)

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Data
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Usage analytics (page views, navigation patterns)
- Cookies and similar technologies

### 1.3 Subscription & Institutional Data
For Premium and Institutional tiers, we may collect:
- Organization name
- Billing details
- User access logs (for security and compliance)

We do **not** collect sensitive personal data unless explicitly required and voluntarily provided.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

We use personal data for:

- Providing access to content and subscription services
- Sending newsletters or updates (opt‑in only)
- Processing payments
- Improving platform performance and security
- Preventing fraud or misuse
- Complying with legal obligations

We do **not** sell personal data.

---

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process personal data under the following legal bases:

- **Consent** (newsletter signup, cookies)
- **Contractual necessity** (subscriptions, account access)
- **Legitimate interest** (security, analytics)
- **Legal compliance** (record‑keeping, fraud prevention)

Users may withdraw consent at any time.

We use cookies to:

- Maintain session functionality
- Analyze platform usage
- Improve user experience
- Support subscription features

Users may disable cookies through browser settings, though some features may not function properly.
